CREATURE FX

Course Duration: 1 year

This course focuses on all the aspects of Creature FX, which includes Cloth, Hair and Muscle/Skin simulation. Throughout the course, we would focus on learning fundamentals, aesthetics, and observation skills along with the technical aspect of simulation. We would touch base on areas such as topology, rigging, animation and scripting as well, which would help us build a smart and smooth workflow.

3D CHARACTER ANIMATION

Course Duration: 1 year

This course focuses on all the aspects of 3D Character Animation, starting from basic animation principles, understanding of weight and use of timing and spacing to show personality and emotion, simple and stylized walk cycle, Strong poses and understanding of body mechanics, Gestures, Expressions & Lip Sync to finesse in acting and Character performance. At the end of the course, you would create a 3D Character Animation showreel under the guidance of your Mentor.

FX

Course Duration: 1 year

This course helps you understand the true power and potential of procedural, node-based workflow techniques of Houdini to create effects in Films such as destruction, explosions, and water simulations. In this course, you will learn to model, texture, simulate and render VFX shots from scratch. At the end of your course, you will be able to create Feature Film quality Effects as part of your showreel.

VFX COMPOSITING

Course Duration: 1 year

This program will prepare you both creatively and technically to become a VFX compositor in the film industry. You would learn to master the VFX process like roto and roto paint, keying, matchmoving and compositing techniques used by VFX studios. At the end of the program, you will be able to create a showreel which involves compositing an entire short VFX scene and showcasing the different passes as part of the VFX Breakdown.
